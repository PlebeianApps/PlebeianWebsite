The NedCompost facility is two 1,000 gallon Earthtubs

The NedCompost composting facility comprises two 1,000 gallon Earthtubs, two bicycle trailers, ~100 5 gallon buckets, and 3 bicycles. Each earthtubs can process up to 150lbs of biomass per day. They are basically large cyclindrical composters with a motorized auger mixer. The auger runs on a track on the lid of the earthtub and the lid must be rotated by a human walking around it to thoroughly mix the compost. A full batch of compost can be processed every 20-40 days.
