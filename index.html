Golden Mind Meditation Timer@PlebeianApps

This meditation timer can provide a container for your meditation, yoga, tai chi, and many other such core practices of contemplative learning and engagement with the world. You can also use it for meetings & other timed practices.

Features:

•Beautiful meditation time-setting dial.
•Wide selection of meditation images & mandalas.
•Image fade out in conjunction with remaining time. This is a new time-tracking function which allows you to get a sense for how much time you have remaining in your meditation session without engaging your linear mind.
•A variety of bell patterns including the "Golden Bells" which ring in gradually increasing frequency according to the golden ratio as your meditation approaches completion.
•An 'About' page which includes
-some tips about the integration of meditation with technology
-some suggestions for how to use this timer
-info about the golden mean


Please enjoy this timer and let us know how you like it as we will be making periodic updates.
